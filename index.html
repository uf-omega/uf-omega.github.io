* The UNDERFELL take with morality.

"Hellfire scorches all."

UNDERTALE has gotten a surge in super creative fan takes and AUs recently.

A few examples being TS!UNDERSWAP, Undertale Yellow, and even SwApFell I hate you.

However, it is becoming increasingly apparent to me just how quickly it is dying back down.

As such, I figured this was the perfect time to create a SoundCloud Account and design a gigantic UNDERFELL take inspired by Inverted Fate with a semi-original storyline and non-linear role changes.

The current plan is to release tracks on the SoundCloud account while continuing the game's development in the background.

As the game progresses, I'll add to this website with new information.
