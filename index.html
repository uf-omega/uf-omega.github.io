* The UNDERFELL take with morality.

"Hellfire scorches all."

UNDERFELL OMEGA is an UNDERTALE fangame created out of the idea that UNDERFELL takes in general change characters completely, removing all sense of morality.

I'm not saying this is bad or ruins every take- Look at Fella's original concept, it's great- I just like how recent fangames try to stay close to those original characterizations, and figured UNDERFELL could use a douse of that as well.

Of course, this is much easier said than done- UNDERFELL is famous for the so-called "edgy" versions of UNDERTALE characters, not so much the clever ideology behind it.

Which in this case would be thee idea to make UNDERTALE truer to classic RPGs.

So to do this, I incorporated a few linear role-changes in the fashion of UNDERSWAP, STORYSHIFT, STORYSPIN, etc etc etc... With a ton of non-linear role changes and original roles, plus new characters and a completely different mountain to fall under.

Oh yeah, and a completely reworked storyline that throws you more screwballs than I have time to count at the present moment.

The current plan is to release tracks on the SoundCloud account while continuing the game's development in the background.

As the game progresses, I'll add to this website with new information, and a new design as well, because as you can see it's...

A bit raw right now.
