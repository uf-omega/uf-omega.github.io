* An UNDERFELL take.

"Another tale."

UNDERFELL OMEGA is an UNDERTALE fangame that strives to be both canon-accurate and unique.

Of course, this is much easier said than done- UNDERFELL is famous for the so-called "edgy" versions of UNDERTALE characters, and changing this would ruin the whole point.

As such, this take focuses swapping concepts like UNDERFELL was originally intended to do.

Even so, most characters will be in different roles and new characters will be added to complement them. And there's... what, four new areas? Well, that, and there's a completely reworked storyline to fit with the whole concept-swapping thing.

The current plan is to release tracks on the SoundCloud account while continuing the game's development in the background.

As the game progresses, I'll add to this website with new information, and a new design as well, because as you can see it's...

A bit raw right now.
